Ticketing management sofware products

In-house developments for Onebox, the company with one of the most advanced ticket distribution system. I was commited to develop some of the most complex developments in order to have internal tools which provide an advantatge over other existing platforms.

In order to achieve that, I developed a full featured visual editor for the creation of venues and all the related business logic. Also a desktop client software was released to manage the ticketing sales done in real time in Point-of-Sales (POS).
